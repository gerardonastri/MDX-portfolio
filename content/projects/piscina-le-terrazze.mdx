Piscina Le Terrazze is an elegant, responsive website built to showcase the breathtaking pool and terrace overlooking the Amalfi Coast. The site offers full bilingual support (Italian and English), a modern interface powered by Tailwind CSS, and smooth animations courtesy of Framer Motion.

In addition to the core website, I integrated a flexible booking system using Supersaas, complete with Stripe payment processing, ensuring guests can reserve their spot and pay securely in just a few clicks.

## Features

1. **Bilingual Content**  
   Full localization in Italian and English, allowing visitors from around the world to explore services and amenities in their preferred language.

2. **Stunning Hero & Gallery**  
   A visually striking hero section and photo gallery highlight the pool, terraces, and panoramic sea views, enticing potential guests.

3. **Interactive Animations**  
   Subtle scroll-triggered and hover animations implemented with Framer Motion enhance user engagement without sacrificing performance.

4. **Seamless Booking Integration**  
   Integrated with Supersaas for real-time availability and reservations. Guests can choose dates, times, and add-ons directly on the site.

5. **Secure Stripe Payments**  
   Stripe API integration for safe and reliable online payments, giving users peace of mind when completing their bookings.

6. **Responsive & Mobile-First Design**  
   Carefully optimized layouts for desktops, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring a flawless experience on every device.

7. **Fast Performance**  
   Deployed on Vercel for instant CDN delivery and automatic optimizations, resulting in lightning-fast load times.

## Technologies

- **Next.js**: Server-side rendering and static generation for optimal performance and SEO
- **MongoDB**: NoSQL database for storing dynamic content and managing availability data
- **Tailwind CSS**: Utility-first CSS for rapid UI development and consistent styling
- **Framer Motion**: High-performance animations and transitions
- **Supersaas**: Cloud-based booking platform for real-time scheduling
- **Stripe**: Payment gateway for secure online transactions
- **Vercel**: Hosting platform for seamless deployments and global CDN


## Conclusion

Piscina Le Terrazze represents the perfect blend of aesthetic beauty and functional technology. By combining a clean, user-centered design with robust booking and payment systems, this project provides both a seamless user experience and an efficient management tool for the business. It's an example of how thoughtful development can elevate a local experience—like a pool day on the Amalfi Coast—into a professional and accessible digital service.
